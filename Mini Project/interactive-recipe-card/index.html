Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins Serves: 8
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
